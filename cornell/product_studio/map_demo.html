Data Center Energy Efficiency demo

Explore various aspects of energy data through our interactive maps and charts. Below are some key topics you can dive into:

  • Estimated CO2 Emissions (tonnes/MWh) - Discover the carbon footprint of energy production across different U.S. states.
  • Average Retail Price of Electricity ($/MWh) - Compare the cost of electricity across different U.S. states.

This content-focused demo contains information sourced from the EIA's electricity data browser, and uses the following fields:

  • Average retail price of electricity
  • Consumption for electricity generation for coal
  • Consumption for electricity generation for natural gas
  • Net generation for all fuels (utility-scale)
